Spanish Course Levels

Learning Spanish quickly, dynamically, and effectively is possible thanks to the Callan Method, a technique designed to help you think and speak directly in the language without mental translation. Our Spanish course is divided into six progressive levels, designed to help you speak from day one and progress confidently.

Callan Method Stages

Discover the progressive stages of the Callan Method. Each level builds on practical skills in grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and fluency.

Here's what you'll learn at each stage

Level 1 – Spanish Basics

Ideal for beginners.

  • Greetings, introductions, simple phrases, and pronunciation.
  • Verbs “ser”, “estar”, “tener” and basic vocabulary.

Level 2 – First Conversations

  • More fluent communication.
  • Full questions and answers, regular/irregular verbs, prepositions, and pronouns.

Level 3 – Past and Description

  • Retelling experiences.
  • Past tenses and descriptions.
  • Connectors like “because”, “but”.

Level 4 – More Tenses and Structures

  • Past imperfect, future, reflexive verbs, and comparisons.
  • More detailed stories and plans.

Level 5 – Advanced Expression

  • Subjunctive, conditional, and complex structures.
  • Opinions, wishes, and doubts.
  • Vocabulary related to health, work, and technology.

Level 6 – Improvement and Fluency

  • Advanced conversation, common mistakes, and indirect speech.
  • Idiomatic expressions and compound tenses.
